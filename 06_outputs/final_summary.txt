# Brew Haven Café – AI SEO Content Cluster Project (Final Summary)

## 🎯 Project Objective
To design an AI-powered SEO Content Marketing system for a café business using keyword research, clustering, content mapping, blog generation, and UGC ad creation.

---

## 📌 Project Workflow Completed

### 1. Keyword Research
- Collected primary and long-tail keywords
- Identified search intent (informational, navigational, transactional)

### 2. Keyword Clustering
- Grouped keywords into logical SEO clusters:
  - Café Discovery
  - Study Cafés
  - Aesthetic Cafés
  - Budget Cafés

### 3. Content Mapping Strategy
- Mapped each keyword cluster to:
  - Blog titles
  - Target keywords
  - SEO meta descriptions

### 4. AI Blog Generation
- Created a 1500+ word SEO pillar blog:
  - “Top 10 Cafés in Chennai You Must Visit in 2026”
- Optimized with relevant keywords and structure

### 5. UGC Ad Creation
- Designed multiple social media ad variations:
  - Emotional hooks
  - Problem-solution format
  - Aesthetic storytelling ads
